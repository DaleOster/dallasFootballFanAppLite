Browser help:


- To subscribe to push notifications, you must have chrome set as your default browser - or be able to select it with the "JUST ONCE" option.

- If chrome is your default browser, you do not have to make any changes.

- If you have a different browser set as your default browser, you can go to Settings -> Applications -> Default Applications. From here, choose the "CLEAR" option next to your browser in the list. Now you are able to either select the "JUST ONCE" option or select chrome as your default browser with the "ALWAYS" options (after clicking "Push Notifications").


To subscribe:


- Click "Push Notifications", click "Subscribe/Unsubscribe", click "CONTINUE", click "Allow"


To unsubscribe:


- Click "Push Notifications", click "Subscribe/Unsubscribe", click the lock to the left of the url in the address bar, click "SITE SETTINGS", click "Notifications", click "Block"


To re-subscribe (if you previously unsubscribed):


- Click "Push Notifications", click "Subscribe/Unsubscribe", click the lock to the left of the url in the address bar, click "SITE SETTINGS", click "Notifications", click "Allow"


Getting back to the main page after subscribing or unsubscribing from push notifications:


- Simply use your device's back button to return to the main page of the app.
